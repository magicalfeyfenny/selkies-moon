# Portrait Production Briefs

These briefs governed the candidate-selection pass and remain the design
contract for the accepted KRA masters in this directory. The original sketch
or sprite is the design authority. Earlier generated portraits are deliberately
excluded as references so their accidental redesigns do not propagate.

All candidates are full-body, polished rendered anime game portraits with clean
linework and controlled cel shading. The complete silhouette, props, hands, and
feet must fit inside a simple solid chroma background. Every candidate must keep
the pose, composition, proportions, facial character, and overall drawing style
of the original sketch or sprite. Candidate variation is limited to faithful
rendering interpretation, line finish, material treatment, and color balance;
it must not change the pose or redesign the character.

## Aster

- Use only the left character in `Selkie's Moon ~ until we meet again ~/art/original_character_references/aster-original.jpeg`.
- Preserve her relatively wide head, short layered hair, freckles, dog ears,
  dog tail, ornate dress, bows, chains, and bunny-headed staff.
- Hair is lavender. Skin is a warm darker tan. Dog-ear interiors use only a
  restrained dusty rose, with substantially less pink than earlier renders.
- Shoulders are exposed. Dress fabric is more sheer and airy while remaining
  non-explicit; gentle purple replaces off-white fabric.
- The large shape behind her is a ribbon, never a wing.
- She holds the complete staff. Its lower blade has a winged tip.
- No stray frill layer beneath the skirt. Frills remain at the skirt edge and
  must be present at the tops of both socks.
- Each shoe has a single tidy double-loop bow, not nested or tripled bows.
- Plush bunny mouth has exactly one stitched X.

## Mira

- Use only the left character in `Selkie's Moon ~ until we meet again ~/art/original_character_references/mira-original.jpeg`.
- Preserve the bob haircut, spotted/poker dress silhouette, fitted zipped
  jacket, shoulder accents, corset seams, gloves, skirt chips, chains, bows,
  and visible card. Do not borrow the hammer from the page.
- Hair is warm medium auburn, close to Aisha's color. Costume is poker-table
  green and black with gold trim and controlled red accents.
- Jacket closes fully to the branching collar; no cleavage is exposed.
- Collar visibly contains exactly one heart, club, diamond, and spade.
- Card grip: index finger is completely behind the card and occluded; middle
  finger is the only finger visibly crossing in front.
- Every hanging skirt chain ends in a gem. Viewer-right skirt emblem is a red
  heart with a red faceted diamond behind/below it.
- Keep center skirt frills complete and use only one symmetric garter per leg.

## Shalmii

- Use `Selkie's Moon ~ until we meet again ~/art/original_character_references/shalmii-original.jpeg` as the design authority.
- Preserve large twin-tails, cropped geometric top, asymmetrical single
  shoulder plate, choker, loose trousers, long rear/side skirt panel, gauntlets,
  boots, and enormous hammer.
- Deep pinks and blacks with silver edges; warm medium-tan skin.
- No animal ears. Exactly two Aerith-like antenna locks sprout from the crown,
  rise, then bend downward. No side or twin-tail antenna.
- Shoulder plate has a visible fastening strap.
- Waist armor/panel assembly goes continuously around her hips; the rear panel
  is visible in the gap between her legs. No stray armor shard by a gauntlet.
- Hammer head is a six-sided hexagonal prism, flat on every side, with no spike
  or pointed tip. Broad faces carry wispy glowing magenta runes.
- No unexplained dangling hand ornament.

## Aisha

- Use `Selkie's Moon ~ until we meet again ~/art/original_character_references/aisha-original.jpeg`; preserve the energetic pose,
  short auburn hair, cape, fitted bodysuit, belt, tall boots, short sword, and
  single paper talisman.
- White complete cape, gray bodysuit, black boots, silver belt, auburn hair.
- Heterochromia: one pink eye and one light-blue eye.
- Two head ornaments: a jagged sinister maroon chaos shard hangs above a
  straight thin blue order crystal. Both glow subtly.
- Necklace has a large blue crystal surrounded by a gold spiral, with a small
  round charm hanging from the bottom.
- Blade of Desires is short, curved like a scimitar/kukri, and narrow at the
  hilt; a lily-of-the-valley motif is carved into the metal.
- Belt also bears a correct lily-of-the-valley emblem.
- The off hand holds exactly one thin paper talisman, never a book, with a
  floating magic circle. Hands have exactly five fingers.

## Caelia

- Use `Selkie's Moon ~ until we meet again ~/art/original_character_references/caelia-original.jpeg` and ignore the photographed pen.
- Preserve the large curled forelock, huge layered hair, puffed asymmetrical
  sleeves, bow, short astral dress, matching boots, and astrolabe-like wand.
- Blue/purple/pink/yellow astral palette. Hair is deep purple with prominent
  light-blue wisps/highlights.
- The rounded dress ends at mid-thigh where it curves inward. Bare thighs show
  before symmetrical boots; boot details match left to right.
- Sleeve gold accents follow plausible seams. Viewer-right shoulder frill is
  intentionally much larger than the left and reads as a design feature.
- Only the two lowest flowing shapes are fabric ribbons; all higher strands are
  unmistakably hair.
- Wand head is a five-point star inside a complete circular cage/ring that
  fully surrounds the large star globe from every side.

## Selkie

- Use `Selkie's Moon ~ until we meet again ~/art/original_character_references/selkie-original.png` as the authority for design,
  colors, silhouette, and pose. Use the five pencil references for Aster, Mira,
  Shalmii, Aisha, and Caelia as the authority for the artist's drawing style,
  proportions, faces, ornament density, and line language. Extrapolate a
  complete full body while retaining her face, silhouette, spear, spiral orange
  hair, layered mantle, bright blouse, pleated skirt, and warm sunrise identity.
- The two pale flowing shapes are satin ribbons, never tails or hair.
- Skirt fades smoothly from blouse blue through violet into coral pink.
- Orange mantle fabric covers the entire mantle body. Dark navy fabric sits
  above it and completes the collar rather than appearing trapped underneath.
- Red tones in the hair should be integrated as a natural sunrise gradient or
  subtle accent rather than a separate awkward red patch.
- Earrings and both irises use a readable sunrise gradient.

## Moon

- Use `Selkie's Moon ~ until we meet again ~/art/original_character_references/moon-original.png` as the authority for design, colors,
  silhouette, and pose. Use the five pencil references for Aster, Mira,
  Shalmii, Aisha, and Caelia as the authority for the artist's drawing style,
  proportions, faces, ornament density, and line language. Extrapolate a
  complete full body while retaining her layered blue-gray hair, crescent
  necklace, crystal, asymmetrical cloak, short dark skirt, stockings, and calm
  night identity.
- Correct natural cloth folds and hands.
- Each violet motif on her arm has five petals/leaves.
- Integrate one white rose into the cloak near its fastening.
- Both irises use a readable sunset gradient.
